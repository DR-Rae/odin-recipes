Stuffed Italian Bread

Description

Stuffed Italian Bread is a delicious and savory dish that combines the flavors of Italian herbs, cheese, and various fillings. It is typically made by hollowing out a loaf of Italian bread and filling it with a mixture of ingredients such as meats, vegetables, and cheeses. The filled bread is then baked until golden brown and crispy on the outside, while remaining soft and flavorful on the inside.

This dish is perfect for parties, gatherings, or as a hearty meal. It can be served as an appetizer, side dish, or even a main course. The versatility of the fillings allows for endless variations, making it a favorite among many.

Ingredients

  • Â½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 loaf Italian bread
  • 12 ounces shredded Italian cheese blend

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Combine butter, parsley, olive oil, and garlic in a bowl.
  3. Slice loaf on the diagonal, about every 2 inches, starting on top of loaf to the bottom without cutting all the way through. Repeat on the opposite diagonal to create cube-shaped sections still attached to the bottom crust.
  4. Spread butter mixture carefully between bread sections; followed by cheese. Wrap loaf in aluminum foil.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven 15 minutes. Unwrap loaf; continue baking until cheese melted, about 10 minutes more.
